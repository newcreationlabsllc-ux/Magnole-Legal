Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 7, 2026

At Magnole, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the data you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered transaction coordinator assistant ("Maggie").

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we collect and process the following information:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and authentication details when you sign up using Google OAuth.
  • Transaction Data: Real estate transaction documents, contracts, forms, and communication logs that you share or cc Maggie on.
  • Communication Data: Email and SMS text communication history associated with your active real estate transactions.
  • Calendar Data: Access to your calendar timelines to automatically update contract milestones and events.

2. How We Use Google User Data

Magnole utilizes Google OAuth for secure authentication and application integration. Our use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

  • We only access the specific scopes required to authenticate your profile, read transaction-related emails you direct to us, and sync dates with your calendar.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your Google user data to third parties.

3. Data Security & Retention

We implement strict industry-standard technical measures to secure your data against unauthorized access. Transaction data is stored securely using cloud database infrastructure and is retained only as long as necessary to manage your active real estate transactions or as required by law.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@magnole.com.
